Full-Stack Development

Narrator.ai

Data Analysis

"Narrator is the first customer data analytics platform built for product teams that uses all of your data directly from your data warehouse."

Narrator.ai is a powerful analytics tool that allows businesses to query and visualize large swaths of their client data. From user flows, to CSR ticket integration, to email follow ups, Narrator paints a detailed picture of who their users are and how they move throught their client's businesses.

I came on board as a contractor and helped build debugging capabilties to ensure quality results. I also added AWS S3 query functionality as well as contributed to the dashboard's UI. This company provides an incredibly powerful and useful tool for business analytics. Although I no longer am contracted with them, I highly recommend you contact them if you are looking for deeper data insights.

Technologies I used for Narrator:

  • Ruby on Rails

The Internet Freedom Festival

Conference Management Platform

"The IFF nurtures collective efforts which, through diversity, inclusivity, and collaboration, support online freedom of expression, protection from digital threats, and expanded access to online spaces."

Using Ruby on Rails and open source technology I built, deployed, and maintain the Internet Freedom Festival's Conference Management Platform. This portal has already been a huge success! We’ve already seen a 17% increase in event submissions in comparison to last year and so many people have signed up that we reached our venue’s maximum capacity!

From a user’s perspective, I’ve created user registration forms, event submission’s, grant application forms, conditional views, and, because the platform has been so successful, a waitlist for all future registered users. From an Admin’s perspective, I added user/event statistics, CSV downloads, event rating systems, limited-access admin users, and customization of user/event profiles.

The IFF Portal is largely built on:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • SQL
  • Frab/Frab
  • HAML
  • Ubuntu / Nginx

Grupo Bilbao Berria

Corporate Restaurant Conglomerate

Grupo Bilbao Berria is a global restaurant conglomerate with restaurants located in Spain, London, Mexico, and the Balearic Islands. As the lead developer for this project, I launched a new web experience based on their business requirements. The site is designed in three languages (Spanish, English, and Catalan), allows for user subscriptions, resume submissions, and represents seven different brands.

The client originally hosted their site on WordPress, but they were having issues keeping their plugins current and updating their content. I switched to Squarespace's CMS to allow for future client-side content updates and added my own custom CSS and JavaScript to create a unique user experience.

GBB's site is primarily built with:

  • Squarespace
  • MailChimp
  • JavaScript
  • JQuery
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Google Analytics

Beso Beach

Corporate Website

Beso Beach is a high-end restaurant chain located in Formentera and Tulum. I completely redesigned their client-facing website, allowing for both English and Spanish navigations, newsletter subscriptions, and I installed Google Analytics to allow for SEO optimization and more focused client stories.

Beso Beach wanted to update their website both stylistically and functionally, since many of their WordPress plugins had become outdated. They wanted a site that they could easily change simple content, like text and pictures, and they wanted to stop monitoring plugins and updates. I decided to move them over to SquareSpace to allow for easy content management, automatic updates, and I used custom CSS and JavaScript to give it a unique feel.

Beso Beach is built on:

  • Squarespace
  • MailChimp
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Google Analytics
