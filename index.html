The Internet Freedom Festival

User/Admin Portal

"The IFF nurtures collective efforts which, through diversity, inclusivity, and collaboration, support online freedom of expression, protection from digital threats, and expanded access to online spaces."

Using Ruby on Rails and open source technology I build, deploy, and maintain the Internet Freedom Festival's User/Admin portal. This portal has already been a huge success! We’ve already seen a 17% increase in event submissions in comparison to last year and so many people have signed up that we reached our venue’s maximum capacity!

From a user’s perspective, I’ve created user registration forms, event submission’s, grant application forms, conditional views, and, because the platform has been so successful, a waitlist for all future registered users. From an Admin’s perspective, I added user/event statistics, CSV downloads, event rating systems, limited-access admin users, and customization of user/event profiles.

The IFF Portal is largely built on:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • Sqlite3
  • Frab/Frab
  • HAML
  • Ubuntu / Ngninx

Grupo Bilbao Berria

Corporate Restaurant Conglomerate

Grupo Bilbao Berria is a global restaurant conglomerate with restaurants located in Spain, London, Mexico, and the Balearic Islands. As the lead developer for this project, I completely redid their corporate website. The site is designed in three languages (Spanish, English, and Catalan), allows for user subscriptions, resume submissions, and represents seven different brands. I was also responsible for translating the Spanish version into English. My wife, Judith Masoni, was indispensable with her contributions to content selection and wrote much of the original descriptions and captions.

The client originally hosted their site on WordPress, but they were having issues keeping their plugins current and updating their content. I switched to Squarespace's CMS to allow for future client-side content updates and added my own custom CSS and event-listeners to create a unique User Experience, separating it from other template-based websites.

GBB's site is primarily built with:

  • Squarespace
  • MailChimp
  • JavaScript
  • JQuery
  • HTML5
  • CSS3

EthiCapital

An ESG based, investment platform.

I built EthiCapital as a platform for both Investors and Financial Advisors to meet and start a financial relationship. An Investor can fill out a questionnaire and EthiCapital will select an investment model that matches their risk tolerance. All investments are based on actual ESG stocks and bonds. (ESG stands for Environmental, Social, and Goverance... basically ethical investments). Clients can search for Advisors based on their location, area of focus, or both and, once they've established a relationship, the two parties can download, sign, and upload client/advisor contracts.

I designed EthiCapital as my Actualize Capstone Project. My next goal with EthiCapital is to redirect image uploads and portfolio submission to AWS S3.

EthiCapital is largely built on:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • PostgreSQL
  • Google Maps API
  • Google Charts
  • Wicked_pdf
  • CarrierWave
  • Heroku
